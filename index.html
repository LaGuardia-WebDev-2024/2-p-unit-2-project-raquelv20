Welcome to The Park

The park is a fictional place that is the main setting of the Cartoon Network show Regular Show. The main place to go to in the park is Pop's House which you will find in the center of the park. Features of Pop's house include Mordecai and Rigby's room, Pop's room, the roof, the computer room, and the kitchen.

Getting around

The main method of transportation in the park is a golf cart. Your tour guides of the park will be Mordecai and Rigby. Mordecai is a blue jay bird and Rigby is a racoon. Although their main jobs are groundskeepers they have no problem helping you find your way around.

Things to do

As long as Mordecai and Rigby are your guides there will never be a dull moment. Although they both have jobs at the park working is something you will rarely see them do. Many of their excursions include learning Death Kwon Do, traveling to the moon with a magic keyboard, prank calling the Master Prank Caller, chugging 'brain juice', and eating so much junk food Rigby's body detached itself from him leaving him as a bodiless consciousness.
